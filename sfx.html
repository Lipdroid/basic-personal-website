Sound Design & Audio Engineering

What I Do

Crafting immersive audio experiences through innovative sound design and precise audio engineering. From cinematic soundscapes to interactive audio for games and applications.

Sound Design

Creating unique and memorable audio elements that enhance storytelling and emotional impact. Specializing in cinematic sound effects, foley, and ambient soundscapes.

Audio Engineering

Professional mixing and mastering services for music, podcasts, and multimedia projects. Ensuring pristine audio quality with industry-standard techniques.

Interactive Audio

Implementing dynamic audio systems for games and interactive experiences. Working with middleware like FMOD and Wwise to create adaptive soundtracks.

Post-Production

Complete audio post-production for film, television, and digital media. Dialogue editing, ADR, sound effects, and final mix for broadcast standards.

Music Production

Composing and producing original music across various genres. From orchestral scores to electronic soundtracks and ambient compositions.

Audio Restoration

Cleaning up and restoring audio recordings using advanced noise reduction and repair techniques. Breathing new life into vintage and damaged recordings.
